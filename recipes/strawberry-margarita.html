Frozen Strawberry Margarita

5 Ingredient Summer Cocktail Guaranteed to Impress Your Friends

On a hot summer's day, there are few things more refreshing than a cool frozen strawberry margarita. Whether enjoyed poolside, with chips and salsa, or simply on your front porch, this strawberry margarita is sure to knock your socks off with its tasty flavors. All you need is five minutes, a few ingredients, and a trusty blender for these top-rated strawberry margaritas.

What You'll Need

  • 2 cups ice
  • 6 fl oz tequila
  • 2 fl oz triple Sec
  • 8 oz frozen Strawberries
  • 4 fl oz limeade Concentrate

How to Throw It Together

  1. Fill a blender with ice cubes; pulse until ice is crushed.
  2. Pour in tequila and triple sec, then add strawberries and limeade concentrate. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  3. Pour into four margarita glasses and serve immediately.
